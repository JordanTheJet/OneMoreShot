# Reference Photo List - One More Shot

## Scene 1: Locker Room (First-Person POV)

### 1.1 - Wide Locker Room
- **Setup:** Sit on a bench in a locker room or gym
- **Camera angle:** Look down at the floor between your feet
- **Capture:** Concrete/tile floor, lockers on both sides, overhead fluorescent lights
- **Mood:** Moody, anticipatory, blue-green lighting

### 1.2 - Teammates Walking Past
- **Setup:** Same seated position on bench
- **Camera angle:** Look up toward a bright doorway
- **Capture:** 3-4 people walking past, backlit by doorway light, silhouettes
- **Mood:** Atmospheric, movement blur okay

### 1.3 - Doorway Toss (Jeffrey)
- **Setup:** Someone (Jeffrey, 14yo) standing in a bright doorway
- **Camera angle:** Looking at them from the bench
- **Capture:** Silhouetted figure holding basketball, turned toward camera, about to toss
- **Mood:** Dramatic backlight contrast

### 1.4 - Ball Catch (INTERACTION)
- **Setup:** Basketball being tossed toward camera
- **Camera angle:** First-person, ball coming at you
- **Capture:** Ball mid-air approaching, hands reaching up from bottom of frame, wristband visible
- **Mood:** Intimate, soft focus background

- **Setup:** Empty outdoor basketball court at sunset

- **Mood:** Athletic anticipation, golden hour

### 2.5 - The Shot
- **Setup:** Teen taking jump shot
- **Camera angle:** Dynamic, capture peak of jump
- **Capture:** Perfect shooting form, ball releasing, wristband visible, golden backlight
- **Mood:** Triumphant, emotional peak

## Photo Tips

### Lighting
- **Scene 1:** Cool fluorescent (blue-green tint)
- **Scene 2:** Golden hour (warm orange, long shadows)
- **Scene 3:** Warm indoor evening light (overhead lamp)
- **Scene 4:** Bright gymnasium (slightly overexposed)

### First-Person Shots
- Camera should be at eye level of someone sitting/standing
- No body visible except hands when specified
- Slight natural movement/imperfection is okay

### Character Consistency
- Jeffrey: Black teenage boy, 14 years old, athletic, wristband on left wrist
- Mom: Black woman, early 40s, warm but tired
- Marcus (hands only): Wristband on left wrist

### Wristband
- Same wristband appears throughout
- Worn on LEFT wrist
- Slightly worn/loved appearance
- Key emotional symbol - make sure it's visible in relevant shots
